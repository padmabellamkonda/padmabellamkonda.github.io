Yelp Area Explorer

I built this because when using Yelp it's difficult to quickly see which neighborhoods actually have a high concentration of good restaurants. Scrolling through long lists doesn't help you understand where the best areas are.

This Flutter app visualizes Yelp restaurant data directly on a map so users can instantly see areas with dense clusters of highly rated restaurants.
